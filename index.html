Queen

About Biography

Queen has been crowned the most popular British band ever as the artists
who have spent the longest time in British album charts.

In 1669, two students at Imperial College, a guitarist Brian May and a bass player Tim Staffell, decided to form a group. They placed an invitation for a drummer on
the college notice board, and a dental student Roger Taylor joined them. They called the band Smile.

The band worked hard, making use of every chance to get agead. They played several small gigs at Imperial College, tested a new recording studio called De Lane Lea in return
for free demo tapes.
